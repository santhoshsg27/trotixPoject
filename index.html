About Us

Trotix was founded by Mr. Vignesh Muthuveerappan solely to satisfy his thirst for product development and mechanization. Trotix's focus is to develop innovative and interesting design solutions for challenges both big and small, guaranteeing exceptionally satisfying results. Trotix strives to provide cutting edge technology in the field of robotics and automation and to deliver intelligible and efficient solutions for various engineering problems. Trotix not only provides consultancy services but also prove our technology by manufacturing and delivering it. Trotix constantly embrace new ideas and create several new state-of-the-art products and solutions for all engineering and automation needs.

Trotix is a hive of innovative and creative activities, where it rely on knowledge base - pure and simple - thinking hard about what clients need to succeed and deliver the success in an atmosphere of mutual respect and trust. Trotix has a wide client base from different fields, to mention – government organizations, engineering firms, colleges, universities and research students.
